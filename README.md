## Featured Projects

### Kaizen Web Application
> An internal platform for collecting, reviewing, and tracking continuous-improvement (Kaizen) suggestions across the company.

- Submission workflow with image uploads, approvals, and status tracking.
- Dashboards & charts for managers to monitor participation and impact.
- LINE notifications, Excel exports, and role-based access control.

`React 19` · `TypeScript` · `Express` · `PostgreSQL` · `AWS S3` · `LINE Bot SDK` · `Chart.js` · `Playwright` · `Docker`

---

### FIXA — Asset Management System
> A web app for tracking company assets through their full lifecycle — registration, assignment, audit, and disposal.

- QR-code scanning for fast asset lookup on mobile.
- Bulk Excel import/export and rich reporting.
- Scheduled jobs for audit reminders and inventory checks.

`React` · `TypeScript` · `Vite` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Radix UI` · `Supabase` · `Express` · `Zod` · `node-cron`

---

### Car Booking System
> A self-service car-pool booking app for employees, with maps, schedules, and approval flows.

- Interactive map view with Leaflet for pickup/drop-off locations.
- Calendar-based reservation with conflict detection.
- Driver assignment, approvals, and booking history.

`React` · `TypeScript` · `Vite` · `Tailwind CSS` · `MUI` · `Leaflet` · `Express` · `Supabase` · `JWT`

---

### GS Tech Lunch
> An event dashboard for organizing internal Tech Lunch sessions — RSVPs, agendas, and engagement tracking.

- Registration flow with QR check-in.
- Real-time stats and feedback collection.
- Theming, drag-and-drop scheduling, and responsive UI.

`React` · `TypeScript` · `Vite` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Radix UI` · `MUI` · `Motion` · `Express` · `Supabase`
